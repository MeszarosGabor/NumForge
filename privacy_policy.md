# Privacy Policy — NumForge

**Last updated: 2026-04-29**

## Overview

NumForge is a fully offline number puzzle game. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

## Data Collection

NumForge collects **no data** of any kind. Specifically:

- No personal information
- No device identifiers
- No location data
- No usage analytics
- No crash reporting
- No advertising identifiers
- No third-party SDKs

## Internet Access

NumForge does not require or use an internet connection. All game logic runs entirely on your device.

## Third Parties

NumForge does not integrate any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

## Age Group

NumForge contains no ads, no in-app purchases, no social features, and collects no data, making it safe for all ages.

## Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be published at this URL with a new date above.
